I am a PhD student in the Biology program at Texas A&M University working in Heath Blackmon's lab. My main interests are sexual antagonism as a force in the shaping of sex chromosome evolution. The other area that has my interest is the evolution of circadian mechanisms and their potential role in adaptation. I use a combination of population genetics, genomics, mathematical modelling, and machine learning/deep learning to explore these questions.
